What's in a Name?


The Power of Names: Shaping Lives and Embracing Destiny

Choosing a child's name is a significant decision, as it reflects parents' hopes, dreams, and expectations for their future. In biblical contexts, God often changed names to establish new identities or convey His message. Names can hold various meanings, symbolizing aspects of a person's birth or expressing parents' reactions. Seeking God's guidance in selecting a name is crucial, as names have the power to shape lives and set the course for one's destiny.
